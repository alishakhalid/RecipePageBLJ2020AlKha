Step 1: Prepare yourself

First you have to Preheat the oven and prepare the tray. Then take your ingredients.

Step 2: Cream Butter and Sugar

Start by melting the butter and then stir it until the peaks form, whisk in the sugar until well-combined.

Step 3: Add dry ingredients

Mix the flour, baking powder and add a pinch of Salt. Sieve it and stir to a smooth batter. If you get a smooth batter then you can add as many chocolate chips as you want.

Step 4: Form a nut

Use 2 teaspoons or your hand to place the dough balls in the size of a tree nut on the baking paper.

Step 5: Press the cookie dough

Press gently with your clean hands so that they are about 1 cm high and refrigerate them for 15 minutes. Mostly the cookies fall apart..to prevent that we put our flat pressed cookies in the fridge.

Step 6: Wait
